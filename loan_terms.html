Loan Terms & Conditions

Effective as of 2025 Financial Year

Borrowing Philosophy

Jera Moyie operates as a peer-to-peer lending group. We lend based on mutual trust and the strength of your individual savings. Borrow responsibly to ensure the group's sustainability.

1. Eligibility & Limits

The 3x Rule: A member is eligible to borrow a maximum of three times (3x) their current savings balance. If your savings are KSh 10,000, your maximum limit is KSh 30,000.

Waiting Period: New members must contribute consistently for at least three (3) months before being eligible for their first loan application.

2. Interest Rates & Calculation

Flat Rate: All loans attract a flat interest rate of 1% per month on the principal amount borrowed.

Example Calculation:
Principal: KSh 10,000
Duration: 3 Months
Interest (1% x 10,000 x 3): KSh 300
Total Repayable: KSh 10,300

3. Repayment Schedules

Duration: Standard loans are repayable within 1 to 6 months depending on the amount. Emergency loans are typically repayable within 1 month.

Installments: Payments are made monthly. Failure to pay an installment results in a late penalty of KSh 200 per week delayed.

4. Default & Recovery

Savings Offset: In the event of a default exceeding 60 days, the group reserves the right to offset the outstanding balance using the member's savings.

Suspension: Members with defaulted loans will be suspended from welfare benefits and further borrowing until the account is settled.

By applying for a loan through the Jera Moyie Portal, you agree to abide by these terms. For custom loan requests or extensions, please contact the Group Treasurer.
