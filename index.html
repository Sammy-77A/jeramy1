Who We Are

We are more than just a financial group; we are a family dedicated to the prosperity of every member.

Established in 2005 in Kisumu County, Jera Moyie Self-Help Group has grown from a small neighborhood initiative to a robust financial pillar. We believe in the power of collective saving and transparent lending to lift our members to new financial heights.

Our Core Pillars

We offer tailored financial products designed to meet your short-term needs and long-term goals.

Smart Savings

Flexible saving plans with no hidden fees. Watch your money grow and earn dividends at the end of the year.

Table Banking

Access instant loans up to 3x your savings with flexible repayment terms of 3 months. Low interest rates and quick approval at our weekly Monday meetings.

Social Welfare

We stand by you. Our welfare kitty covers members during bereavement, hospitalization, and emergencies.

Normal Loans

Long-term loans up to KSh 500,000 (2x your savings). Flexible 1-year repayment period with competitive interest rates.

Uwezo Loan

Quick access micro-loans up to KSh 10,000 for urgent needs. Simple qualification and 3-month repayment period.
