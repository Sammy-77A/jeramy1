Group Constitution

Official Guidelines and Governance for Jera Moyie Self-Help Group

Article I: Name & Objective

The name of this organization shall be Jera Moyie Self-Help Group. The primary objective is to promote the socio-economic welfare of its members through systematic savings, affordable credit services, and mutual social support.

Article II: Membership

  • Membership is open to individuals who share the common goal of the group and are willing to abide by these rules.
  • A non-refundable registration fee as determined by the committee must be paid upon joining.
  • Members must maintain active participation in meetings and meet all contribution requirements.

Article III: Savings & Loans

The group operates on a 3x savings multiplier model:

  • Savings: Regular monthly savings are mandatory for all active members.
  • Loan Eligibility: A member is eligible for a loan up to three (3) times their total savings balance.
  • Interest: Loans attract a flat interest rate of 1% per month (or as revised by the general assembly).
  • Guarantors: Large loans may require security or guarantors from within the active membership.

Article IV: Welfare Fund

The Welfare Fund is a collective pool designed to assist members during emergencies:

  • Contributions: A fixed monthly welfare contribution is mandatory.
  • Coverage: Includes bereavement (immediate family), hospitalization, and catastrophic loss.
  • Payouts: Specific payout amounts for each category are reviewed annually by the Executive Committee.

Article V: Meetings & Governance

The group shall be governed by an elected Executive Committee consisting of a Chairperson, Secretary, and Treasurer.

  • General Meetings: Held monthly at a designated venue.
  • Quorum: A meeting is valid only if two-thirds (2/3) of the active membership is present.
  • Elections: Committee members shall serve a term of two years, with a maximum of two consecutive terms.
